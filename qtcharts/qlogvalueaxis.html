详细描述

The QLogValueAxis class is used for manipulating chart's axis.

注:If a QLogValueAxis is attached to a series with one or more points with negative or zero values on the associated dimension, the series will not be plotted at all. This is particularly relevant when XYModelMappers are used, since empty cells in models typically contain zero values.
